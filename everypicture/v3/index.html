chevron_leftAbout this page

This page is a chance to interact with some instances of type and patterns that are seldom paid very much attention. Typography and patterns such as those found on manhole covers access hatches are walked, ran, biked, driven, and jumped on countless times per day. If we take a moment to sit with these pieces, we realize that they are interesting in their own right. Their uniqueness is further accentuated when juxtaposed against one another, as in the interactive mode on this page.

Take a moment to play around with and enjoy these photos!
